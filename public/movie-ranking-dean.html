The Best Movies According to Dean

My top 3 movies of all-time

Django

This is one of my favourite movies. I love Tarantino's style and the twists and turns through the whole movie.

Dune Part 2

Cinematic Masterpiece. Keeps you gripped in through the whole film and all of the action scenes are amazing.

The Dark Knight Rises

This was my favourite movie for a long long time. It is a close one between this one and The Dark Knight but this takes the lead for me (as contraversial as that is).
